Size Guide

Our Sizing and Standard Sizing

Due to limited demand, all of our products follow a "one-size-fits-all" standard. Please note that for necklaces and bracelets, the adjustable chain accomodates all sizes. Our sizing is as follows:

  • Ring Size - UK Size J
  • Necklace Size - M
  • Bracelet Size - M
