About Bejewel

Jenny Thao Huynh founded Bejewel in 2017 at the age of 18 during her studies in university. The Dublin native initially began handcrafting bead jewellery with seed beads from her local bead shop, Yellow Brick Road, during her bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. She began with floral rings, pearl chokers and other pieces of jewellery and posted her work on Instagram. After gaining popularity, she shifted away from her studies to set up an ecommerce website for her jewellery and is now expanding to brick-and-mortar stores.

Bejewel has several goals in mind, such as:

  1. Create feminine, delicate and handmade jewellery that add a princess-like touch to any outfit.
  2. Empower wearers to be comfortable in their femininity.
  3. Inspire wearers to choose sustainable and long-lasting jewellery made to last an entire lifetime from small businesses.

How Our Jewellery's Made

Seed bead jewellery isn’t something that Bejewel invented and since our goal is to encourage wearers to choose sustainable jewellery, we’d love for our customers to start creating their own too. There are several resources online demonstrating how to create your own jewellery such as Youtube tutorials.
